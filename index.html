About Me

I am Navya, a passionate Data Scientist with 5 years of experience turning complex data into meaningful insights. With a Master's in Business Analytics from the University of Texas at Dallas, I specialize in SQL, Python, Machine Learning, AI, and am currently diving into GenAI, Tableau, Power BI, Power Query, and VBA. Having spent 3 years as a Data Scientist at Applied Materials, I’ve worked on high-impact projects that blend analytics with innovation.

Beyond algorithms and dashboards, I am deeply fascinated by industries like retail, fashion, skincare, mental health, pharmaceuticals (Oncology), product-based businesses, trading, finance, and e-commerce. I believe in using data as a force for good—whether it’s optimizing business strategies, decoding consumer behavior, or driving advancements in healthcare.

Giving back to the data community has always been close to my heart, and I strive to make the world a better place through my work. I am also passionate about social impact and open to joining an NGO to contribute toward a better future for the next generations. Let's harness the power of data to innovate, empower, and drive meaningful change!
